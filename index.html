Welcome! We are glad you are visiting Voltage Coffee and we hope you stick around.

A little about us, we are a group of coffee lovers from all around the world. We have been brewing, pressing, and even self-roasting for a combined 100 years. The inspiration for Voltage Coffee came from our desire to give back to the coffee community. We see too many coffee drinkers settling for subpar beans, makers, and methods. Once people “see the light” of good coffee, there is no going back!

On Voltage Coffee, we review the best coffee makers, espresso machines, single-serve makers, and beans. Not only that, we provide DIY guides and short videos that will help any casual coffee drinker become better acquainted with the coffee enthusiast world. There is too many blogs out there catering to either the extreme enthusiast (also known as a coffee snob, which we certainly are!), or to complete beginners. The Voltage Coffee mission is to educate, entertain, and serve the large enthusiast population. These are the folks who care about drinking a good cup of coffee, but have a life too!

Have a look around, read some reviews, and give us some feedback! We welcome your comments and suggestions, and we are always on the lookout for the next best thing in coffee.
